About Omech

Omech is a 35 year old well established, ISO Certified group involved in Precision Engineering Activities of Tool Room Automotive Components. We are manufacturers of a wide assortment of Office,Institutional, Commercial and Residential Furniture. Our core strength is Furniture custom made to Design & Specifications. Our belief is in building a relationship of Confidence & Trust with our our Customers with our Quality Products
